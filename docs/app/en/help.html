Help Center

Your comprehensive guide to mastering Chinese with Easy Speak Chinese.

Getting Started

How do I start my first lesson?

Simply navigate to the "Learn" tab on the main dashboard. If you're a complete beginner, we strongly recommend starting with the "Pinyin Basics" module to master the tones and pronunciation foundations before moving to vocabulary.

Is there a placement test?

Yes! Upon your first login, you can opt to take our 10-minute AI-driven placement test. It assesses your listening, reading, and speaking skills to recommend the most suitable HSK level for your start.

Learning Experience

How does voice recognition work?

Our app uses advanced neural networks to analyze your pronunciation against native speaker samples. For the best results, ensure you're in a quiet environment and speak clearly into your device's microphone.

Can I learn offline?

Premium subscribers can download entire modules for offline use. Look for the "Download" icon at the top right of any module page. Your progress will automatically sync once you're back online.

What are 'Daily Streaks'?

Streaks track how many consecutive days you've completed at least one lesson. Maintaining a streak earns you "Panda Points" which can be used to unlock bonus culture modules and special avatars.

Account & Billing

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Profile > Settings > Subscription. From there, you can view your current plan, upgrade to Premium, or manage your renewal settings. Subscriptions managed via the App Store or Google Play must be cancelled through their respective platforms.

Can I sync my progress across multiple devices?

Yes. As long as you log in with the same account (Email, Google, or Apple ID), your progress, streaks, and Panda Points will sync automatically across all iOS and Android devices.

Technical Support

The app is crashing or freezing. What should I do?

First, ensure you are running the latest version of the app from the Store. If problems persist, try clearing the app cache in Settings > Storage > Clear Cache. If that fails, please reach out via the 'Contact Us' button below.
